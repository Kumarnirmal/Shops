How to use:
1. Unzip the folder.
2. Open index.html in your browser.
3. Replace images in the images/ folder with your own photos (keep filenames or update paths).
4. Edit text content in the HTML files to match your studio info.

Notes:
- The contact form uses a simple JavaScript alert for demo. Connect to your backend or form service for real submissions.
- The map iframe in contact.html has an empty src parameter; replace with your Google Maps embed link.
